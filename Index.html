✝ Virtues & Vices Guide

Orbs appear mid-court and activate when the ball passes through them. Effects apply to the last player who touched the ball and last 4 turns (a turn = any paddle hit). The ball also speeds up 1% each turn.

♦ Virtues — Gold Orbs

◆
PrudenceUltra Rare
Slows the ball to 75% speed for 4 turns.
◆
FortitudeRare
Your paddle grows to 3× its default size for 4 turns.
◆
JusticeRare
Opponent paddle shrinks by 50% for 4 turns.
◆
CharityCommon
Ball splits into 20 friendly balls that score for you.
◆
HopeCommon
A second paddle appears to assist you for 4 turns.
◆
FaithCommon
Ball gently curves toward your paddle as it returns to you, for 4 turns.
◆
TemperanceRare
Instantly removes all active vices affecting you.

♦ Vices — Crimson Orbs

✕
SlothRare
Your paddle speed is reduced by 50% for 4 turns.
✕
PrideUltra Rare
Your paddle shrinks to 25% of its default size for 4 turns.
✕
AngerRare
The ball bounces back at 200% speed for 4 turns.
✕
EnvyCommon
Instantly removes all active virtues affecting you.
✕
GluttonyRare
Your paddle is stunned for 2 turns.
✕
LustCommon
Paddle controls are inverted for 4 turns.
✕
GreedCommon
Removes all virtue orbs from the field and clears your active virtues.
