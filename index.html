✝ Virtues & Vices Guide

Orbs appear mid-court and activate when the ball passes through them. Effects apply to the last player who touched the ball and last 4 turns. Pip dots (●●●○) by your paddle show turns remaining. Ball speed grows +1.5% per hit.

♦ Virtues — Gold Orbs

◆
PrudenceUltra Rare
Shows a full trajectory tracker predicting where the ball will land. With Charity: incoming balls slow instead. With Faith: the tracker bends dynamically with the curve.
◆
FortitudeRare
Your paddle grows to 3× its default size for 4 turns.
◆
JusticeRare
Opponent paddle shrinks by 50% for 4 turns.
◆
CharityCommon
Ball splits into 20 balls aimed at the opponent. The round is decided by the last surviving ball.
◆
HopeCommon
A second assist paddle appears beside yours for 4 turns.
◆
FaithCommon
Ball gently curves toward your paddle when returning to you, for 4 turns.
◆
TemperanceRare
Instantly removes all active vices affecting you.

♦ Vices — Crimson Orbs

✕
SlothRare
Your paddle speed is reduced by 50% for 4 turns.
✕
PrideUltra Rare
Your paddle shrinks to 25% of its default size for 4 turns.
✕
AngerRare
The ball bounces back faster on every hit, for 4 turns.
✕
EnvyCommon
Instantly removes all active virtues affecting you.
✕
GluttonyRare
Your paddle is stunned and cannot move for 2 turns.
✕
LustCommon
Paddle controls are inverted for 4 turns.
✕
GreedCommon
Removes all virtue orbs from the field and clears your active virtues instantly.
