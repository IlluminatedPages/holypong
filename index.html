✝ Virtues & Vices Guide

Orbs appear mid-court and activate when the ball passes through them. Effects apply only to the last player who touched the ball.

♦ Virtues — Gold Orbs

◆
Prudence
Slows the ball for 10 seconds — right reason gives you time to think.
◆
Fortitude
Grows your paddle ×2 for 10 seconds — courage and strength in battle.
◆
Justice
Shrinks the CPU paddle for 10 seconds — giving each their due.
◆
Charity
Doubles your points for 10 seconds — love overflows in generosity.
◆
Hope
Shields your streak for 10 seconds — if CPU scores, your streak is preserved.
◆
Faith
Shows an aim guide on the ball for 10 seconds — faith illuminates the path.
◆
Temperance
Instantly cleanses all active Vice debuffs — moderation restores right order.

♦ Vices — Crimson Orbs

✕
Sloth
Slows your paddle for 10 seconds — laziness dulls your response.
✕
Pride
Shrinks your paddle for 10 seconds — pride comes before a fall.
✕
Wrath
Speeds the ball dangerously for 10 seconds — wrath brings chaos.
✕
Envy
Gives the CPU a scoring bonus for 10 seconds — envy benefits your opponent.
✕
Gluttony
Bloats your paddle beyond control for 10 seconds — excess becomes a burden.
✕
Lust
Inverts your left/right controls for 10 seconds — disordered desire confuses the will.
✕
Greed
Steals 500 points immediately — greed takes what it should not have.
