✝ Virtues & Vices Guide

Orbs appear mid-court and are activated when the ball passes through them. Effects apply only to the last player who touched the ball. Gold orbs grant Virtues (buffs). Crimson orbs carry Vices (debuffs).

♦ Virtues — Gold Orbs

◆
Prudence
Slows the ball for 5 seconds — right reason in action gives you time to think.
◆
Fortitude
Grows your paddle for 5 seconds — courage and strength in battle.
◆
Justice
Shrinks the CPU/opponent paddle for 4 seconds — giving each their due.
◆
Charity
Doubles your points for the next 4 seconds — love overflows in generosity.
◆
Hope
Shields your streak for 6 seconds — if CPU scores while active, your streak is preserved.
◆
Faith
Shows a subtle aim guide on the ball for 5 seconds — faith illuminates the path.
◆
Temperance
Instantly cleanses all active Vice debuffs — moderation restores right order.

♦ Vices — Crimson Orbs

✕
Sloth
Slows your paddle movement for 4 seconds — laziness dulls your response.
✕
Pride
Shrinks your paddle for 4 seconds — pride comes before a fall.
✕
Wrath
Speeds the ball dangerously for 4 seconds — wrath brings chaos to the field.
✕
Envy
Gives the CPU a scoring bonus for 5 seconds — envy benefits your opponent.
✕
Gluttony
Bloats your paddle beyond control for 4 seconds — excess becomes a burden.
✕
Lust
Inverts your left/right controls for 3.5 seconds — disordered desire confuses the will.
✕
Greed
Steals 200 points immediately — greed takes what it should not have.
